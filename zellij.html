Zellij Cheatsheet

This is a cheatsheet for my custom Zellij keybinds

Pane mode

C-a Init pane mode

Left Move focus left

Right Move focus right

Up Move focus up

Down Move focus down

t Tab mode

s Session mode

m Move mode

r Resize mode

C-q Quit

h Move focus left and back to normal

l Move focus right and back to normal

j Move focus down and back to normal

k Move focus up and back to normal

p Switch focus

n New pane

A-l New pane left

A-r New pane right

A-d New pane down

A-u New pane up

x Close pane

f Toggle fullscreen

z Toggle zoom

w Toggle floating panes

e Toggle embed or floating

c Rename pane

Move mode

C-n Switch back to normal

n or Tab Move pane

p Move pane backwards

h or Left Move pane left

l or Right Move pane right

j or Down Move pane down

k or Up Move pane up

Tab mode

C-w Switch back to normal

r Rename tab

h or Left or Up or k Go to previous tab

l or Right or Down or j Go to next tab

n New tab

x Close tab

s Toggle sync

b Break pane

] Break pane right

[ Break pane left

1-9 Go to tab 1 to 9

Tab Toggle tab
